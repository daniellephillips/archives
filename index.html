The Tragic Case of Sharon Schlosser

On October 20th, 1992 Sharon Schlosser left her home in Clackamas, Oregon for Mount Vernon, Washington but failed to reach her destination. Sharon's body, was discovered in the Columbia River on October 27. Below is an overview of the key aspects and figures involved in this tragic case.

A Mysterious Death

Sharon Francis Schlosser's body, discovered in the Columbia River on October 27, remains a perplexing and unresolved mystery. Below is an overview of the key aspects and figures involved in this tragic case.

Sharon Schlosser's Murder

In October 1992, Sharon was tragically found deceased in the Columbia River. Classified as a homicide, the cause of death was attributed to severe head injuries. Suspicion centered around her husband, Charles Schlosser, yet he has not faced any criminal charges. A witness alleged Charles offered him a Corvette and a sum of money to murder Sharon in a manner eerily similar to her actual death. This assertion, combined with substantial circumstantial evidence, casts a shadow of doubt.

Legal Battles and Investigations

The aftermath of Sharon's death saw multiple legal proceedings. Notably, a federal civil trial concerning her life insurance policy ended without a verdict. Additionally, Sharon's family pursued a wrongful death lawsuit against Charles, accusing him of orchestrating her death and attempting to shield his assets from potential civil judgment.

Family's Quest for Justice

Sharon's children from a previous marriage have been at the forefront of seeking justice. Despite the absence of criminal charges, they firmly believe in Charles Schlosser's involvement. Their pursuit of justice is unwavering, fueled by their mother's memory and a hope for eventual criminal accountability.

Continuing Investigation

Decades have passed, yet the investigation into Sharon's murder remains active. Law enforcement continues to regard Charles Schlosser as a prime suspect. Despite multiple grand jury presentations, the evidence necessary for an indictment has remained elusive.

Key Figures in the Case

  • Detective Pieter Van Dyke - Investigated the circumstances of Sharon's death and Charles Schlosser's statements.
  • Detective Karl Hutchison - Involved in the case.
  • Gary Muncy - Sheriff's detective assigned to the case.
  • Sam Hooson - Testified regarding Charles Schlosser's alleged murder proposition involving a Corvette and $2,000.
